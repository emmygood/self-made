Why visit our salon?

We care because you do

Here at 'Self-Made' we understand that our identity is what makes us all unique. and although we know it's what on the inside that really counts, we believe that being comfortable and happy in how you express yourself to others helps us to be the most authentic version of ourselves.

We pride ourselves with the knowledge that a haircut or colour change can majorly boost your confidence - and our clients will agree!

Inclusivity

Whether you're looking to finally get those highlights you've wanted for years or jump in for that complete re-style - our stylists are trained by the best.

We are an all inclusive salon - meaning that we offer haircuts to any member of LGBTQ+ community as well as gender-affirming/gender-neutral haircuts - to a lot of people their hair is a huge part of who they are.
